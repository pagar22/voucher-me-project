About VoucherMe!

VoucherMe provides a platform for businesses to advertise by posting regular updates on their business’s growth/development while also posting exclusive offers on time-limited promo codes, schemes or vouchers. Users can view this information without registering to the web app and can upvote a business’s post if they’re interested in it. This would enable businesses to keep track of their marketing trends and site effectiveness by gaining valuable feedback. Businesses can build their profile to attract more customers by attaching external links to their websites/resources or posting frequently. Businesses can also look at the schemes/advertising strategies of other businesses to get an outlook of their competition and make relevant business decisions thereon.





VoucherMe was created by Harvey Cameron-Sproull and Aaryan Pagar as part of their WAD2 assessed exercise (for which they obviously got an A1).
